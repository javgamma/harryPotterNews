SIRIUS BLACK ESCAPA

Hogwarts, 20 de mayo de 2024
En un giro impactante de los acontecimientos, el famoso prisionero de Azkaban, Sirius Black, ha logrado escapar de la prisión más segura del mundo mágico. Según informes del Ministerio de Magia, Black, quien fue encarcelado hace doce años por el asesinato de Peter Pettigrew y otros doce muggles, se liberó de su celda en una audaz maniobra que ha dejado a las autoridades desconcertadas. La fuga de Black ha provocado un gran alboroto en toda la comunidad mágica, con numerosos aurores y miembros de la Orden del Fénix desplegados en una búsqueda frenética para recapturarlo. Se insta al público a permanecer vigilante y reportar cualquier avistamiento de Black a las autoridades de inmediato.

Gryffindor Wins the Hogwarts Quidditch Cup

Hogwarts, May 18, 2024 - In a final match that kept everyone on the edge of their seats, the Gryffindor Quidditch team secured victory against Slytherin with a score of 250 to 200. Gryffindor's Seeker, Harry Potter, made a spectacular capture of the Golden Snitch, ensuring his team's triumph. The match was filled with excitement and highlights, including an impressive series of goals by Chaser Ginny Weasley and a fierce defense by Keeper Ron Weasley. "It was an incredible match, and I am very proud of the entire team," commented Gryffindor Captain Oliver Wood. Slytherin Coach Severus Snape congratulated Gryffindor on their victory, although some in the crowd noted his usual grim expression. "It was a fair and exciting competition. Congratulations to Gryffindor," Snape stated. The final match, held at the Hogwarts Quidditch pitch, drew a massive crowd of students, professors, and magical creatures alike. The atmosphere was electric, with cheers and chants filling the air as the teams took to the sky. The Gryffindor stands were adorned with red and gold banners, and supporters waved enchanted flags that sparkled in the sunlight.
